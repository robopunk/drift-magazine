Editorial Assessment · March 2026

Sandoz enters 2026 with its Golden Decade narrative largely intact — all six active objectives remain publicly referenced. The biosimilar leadership commitment is the strongest in the tracked portfolio: language has not softened once across nine reporting events. Two objectives show measurable drift: Emerging Markets has migrated from volume leadership to profitability framing without announcement, and Manufacturing Simplification has lost its specific numeric targets. Three objectives have entered the graveyard — one by silent drop, one by morphing, one by phased retirement.
