The design concept

Three things one line communicates

01
Time flows left to right
The horizontal axis is the calendar. Every node is a signal event — the moment a disclosure was classified. The distance between nodes shows the silence between disclosures.
02
Height is momentum
Higher above the ground line means stronger commitment — more specific language, results cited, targets reaffirmed. Sinking toward the line means drifting. Below it means the graveyard.
03
Crossing is the editorial event
The moment an objective's trajectory pierces the ground line downward is when the alert fires, the social post goes out, and the story is written. That crossing is the whole product, made visible.
